Ali Garjani

Hi! I'm Ali Garjani, a master's student studying Computational Science and Engineering at EPFL with a B.S. in Computer Engineering. I've participated in research projects at VILAB, supervised by Amir Zamir, and IVRL, supervised by Sabine Süsstrunk. Moreover, I worked with Deniz Gündüz lab as a research intern during my bachelor's. I'm interested in looking into computer vision problems through theory and use mathematical concepts to tackle them.
