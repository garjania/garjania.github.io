I'm Ali, it seems like I need to professionally introduce myself here, so here I go: I'm a first year PhD student at EPFL in the EDIC program, where I'm fortunate to be advised by Amir Zamir at VILAB and co-advised by Sabine Susstrunk. In terms of research interests, I cannot exactly pinpoint one, but in general I'm interested in building a theoretical understanding of what reality is and how different representations of reality (e.g., different modalities) are related to each other.
